LASAGNA RECIPE

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish. It consists of layers of pasta sheets intermingled with a flavorful meat sauce, béchamel sauce, cheese, and sometimes vegetables. The dish is baked until the pasta layers become tender and the cheese melts and turns golden. Lasagna is renowned for its comforting texture and a rich blend of flavors. It can be adapted in various ways, with variations like vegetarian lasagna, chicken lasagna, spinach lasagna, and many other creative options. It's a versatile dish often served as a comforting homemade meal worldwide.

ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Tomato sauce
  • Herbs and spices
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

preparation steps

  1. Boil the Pasta Sheets: Boil the lasagna sheets until they are partially cooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce: In a skillet, cook ground meat until browned, then add onions, garlic, carrots, and celery. Stir in tomato sauce and herbs. Simmer.
  3. Make the Bechamel Sauce: In a separate pan, melt butter, add flour, and gradually whisk in hot milk. Cook until it thickens, and add a pinch of nutmeg. Set aside.
  4. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  5. Layer the Lasagna: In a baking dish, start layering with a thin coat of meat sauce. Add a layer of pasta sheets, then a layer of bechamel sauce, ricotta or cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a cheese layer on top.
  6. Cover and Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for about 25-30 minutes.
  7. Uncover and Bake: Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until the lasagna is golden and bubbling.
  8. Rest and Serve: Allow it to rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
