chicken stock

Description

Is a flavorful and nourishing liquid made by simmering chicken meat, bones, and vegetables in water. It serves as a versatile base for a wide range of culinary creations, including soups, stews, and sauces. This clear and savory broth adds depth of flavor and a comforting warmth to many dishes, making it a staple in many cuisines around the world.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Chicken bones
  • onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Peppercorns
  • water
  • salt

preparation steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:Gather chicken pieces, chicken bones, onions, carrots, celery, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, water, and salt.
  2. Simmer the Chicken: In a large pot, add the chicken pieces and bones. Cover with water.
  3. Add Vegetables and Seasoning: Add chopped onions, carrots, celery, crushed garlic, bay leaves, a few peppercorns, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Simmer: Bring the pot to a boil, then reduce the heat to a low simmer. Let it cook for 2-3 hours, skimming off any foam that forms on the surface.
  5. Strain the Broth: After simmering, strain the broth to remove solids, leaving only the clear liquid.
  6. Cool and Store: Allow the broth to cool, then store it in containers. It can be used immediately or refrigerated or frozen for future use.
