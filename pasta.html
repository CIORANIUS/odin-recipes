PASTA RECIPE

PASTA PREPARATION METHOD

  1. Boil the Pasta:

    • Fill a large pot with water and add a generous amount of salt (about 1-2 tablespoons). This seasons the pasta as it cooks.
    • Bring the water to a rolling boil.
    • Add the pasta (spaghetti, penne, fusilli, etc.) to the boiling water.
    • Cook according to the package instructions until al dente, typically 8-12 minutes depending on the type of pasta.

  2. Prepare the Sauce:

    • While the pasta is cooking, prepare your sauce. For a simple tomato sauce:

      • Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
      • Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant (about 1 minute).
      • Stir in canned crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce.
      • Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar (to balance the acidity of the tomatoes).
      • Add fresh or dried herbs like basil, oregano, and a bay leaf.
      • Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce:

    • Once the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of the pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta in a colander.
    • Return the pasta to the pot or a large mixing bowl.
    • Pour the sauce over the pasta and toss to combine. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta cooking water a little at a time until the desired consistency is reached.

  4. Serve:

    • Divide the pasta into serving bowls or plates.
    • Top with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil or parsley if desired.
    • Serve immediately with a side of garlic bread or a green salad.
