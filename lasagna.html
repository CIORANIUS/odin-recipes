LASAGNA RECIPE

Lasagna Preparation Method

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

    • Sauté chopped onions and garlic in olive oil until soft.
    • Add ground beef (or pork) and cook until browned.
    • Stir in tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and a blend of Italian seasonings (basil, oregano, salt, and pepper).
    • Simmer the sauce for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  2. Cook the Noodles:

    • Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
    • Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.

  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:

    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, salt, pepper, and chopped parsley or spinach (optional).

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta.
    • Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.

  5. Bake:

    • Cover the dish with aluminum foil (to prevent the cheese from burning) and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  6. Serve:

    • Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing to allow it to set.
    • Serve warm and enjoy!
